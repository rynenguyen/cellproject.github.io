Welcome to Ryne's Cell Game!

Hi! Welcome to my Cell Game. To play, select either the animal or plant cell. Directions:

  1. Select either cell
  2. Read the question
  3. Drag the correct organelle onto the frame at the top of the page. (if you get it wrong it won't go into the frame)
  4. Repeat until all organelles are in the puzzle
  5. Done!
  6. Please press the back button when your're done, thanks!
